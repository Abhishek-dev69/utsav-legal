Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 6, 2026

Welcome to UTSAV. Your privacy is paramount. This policy explains how we collect and protect your data across our platform on both iOS and Android platforms.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect information necessary to facilitate professional event planning and vendor collaboration:

  • Personal Data: Name, Email, Phone Number, and Business details.
  • Event Logistics: Event dates, locations, requirements, and budget details.
  • Operational Data: Staff attendance records and payroll calculations.

📸 1.1 Media, Camera & Files

UTSAV requests access to your device's Camera and Photo Library. We collect and store photos solely for:

  • Setting and updating your professional Profile Picture.
  • Uploading Vendor Portfolio images and videos to showcase services.

2. USE OF DATA

Data is used exclusively to facilitate your event planning workflow, manage vendor payouts, and ensure team transparency. We never sell your data to third-party advertisers.

3. SECURITY

All data is encrypted and stored using Supabase (PostgreSQL) enterprise-grade infrastructure. External access is strictly controlled via row-level security (RLS).

User Rights Policy

Data Deletion & Removal

We respect your right to be forgotten. We provide two ways to permanently remove your data:

1. In-App Deletion

Navigate to Profile > Edit Profile > Delete Account. This will instantly purge your specific user data from our databases.

🌐 2. Web-Based Deletion Request

In accordance with Global App Store Guidelines, you may also request account and data deletion via our public portal if you cannot access the app:

Request Link: Account Deletion Request Page

Requests submitted via the web portal are typically processed within 48-72 hours.
